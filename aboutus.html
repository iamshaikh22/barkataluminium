Overview

With our rich industrial expertise and knowledge, we have earned expertise in manufacturing a range in all type of Aluminium Fabrication products and services including Resincial, Commercial as well as Industrial.

Barkat Aluminium was Incorporated in the year 2008 at Vapi. Our company is engaged in all type of fabrication work product such as Aluminium Fabrication, Aluminium Doors & Windows, Aluminium Sliding Folding Partition, Aluminum Sound Proof Window, Aluminium Cabinet, Aluminium Office Partition, Aluminium Structural Glassing etc...

Barkat Aluminium is also involved Aluminium Work services like Stainless Steel Fabrication, Aluminum Cabinet Service, industrial Aluminium Fabrication and many more.

These are adorned for dimensional accuracy, durability, rust resistance, customized designs and reasonable prices. Further, we also undertake work for Fabrication, Curtain wall, Structural Glazing, Spider Glazing and Aluminum Partition etc. We assure our clients best products and services at unmatched prices.
